Slide 02 // The Problem

People sign contracts they can't read

Freelancers and small teams sign away pre-existing IP, accept unlimited liability, and agree to 90-day payment terms — because a lawyer costs $300–500/hour and takes days.

Hidden liability
One clause can make you personally liable for everything.
Lawyers are slow + costly

Contracts are confidential
You can't just paste them into a public chatbot.
Slide 03 // The Solution

A junior associate that never sleeps

  • ①Splits the contract into numbered clauses, hashed for integrity.
  • ②Risk-rates each clause with the Anna host LLM and drafts a safer alternative.
  • ③You decide — accept, edit, or keep, in a side-by-side review desk.

Clauses are AES-GCM-256 enveloped locally; the final memo is Ed25519-signed for a tamper-proof audit trail.
